# Project Brief

*   **Project Name:** Hyperswitch
*   **Core Goal:** To provide a single, open-source, enterprise-grade payment orchestration platform that simplifies access to the global payments ecosystem for digital businesses. Aims to be the "Linux for Payments".
*   **Key Features:** Unified payment API, diverse payment method support (Cards, Wallets, BNPL, etc.), smart routing (success-based, rule-based, fallback), payment lifecycle management (Auth, Capture, Void, Refund, Chargeback), connector integration (PSPs, FRM, Auth), SDKs (Web, Android, iOS), Control Center (no-code management), secure card vault (Locker), analytics.
*   **Target Audience:** Digital businesses, enterprises, developers looking to manage complex payment stacks or integrate multiple payment providers efficiently.
*   **Scope:** Provide a comprehensive, modular, and customizable payment infrastructure backend, frontend SDKs, and a management UI. Focus on orchestration, not direct payment processing itself (relies on connectors).
*   **Success Metrics:** [To be defined - Likely adoption rate, number of connectors, transaction volume processed, community contributions]
